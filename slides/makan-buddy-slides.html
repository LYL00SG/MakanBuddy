The Problem

“Where should I eat?”

For anyone in Singapore who's hungry and can't decide.

  • Too many options — hawker, cafe, restaurant, street food — and the scene changes weekly.
  • The right pick is personal — location, craving, dietary (halal · veg · no pork).
  • Search & maps overwhelm — you just want one trustworthy shortlist.

Why AI

Conversation handles vague asks, remembers context, and turns live web results into a short, sourced shortlist — a list or keyword search can't.

What I Learned

Keeping an AI honest is the real work

Harder than expected

The model fabricated sources, ratings, even halal tags. Fixed with a structured schema, strict prompt rules, and code that drops fake URLs.

Do differently

Go web-first from day one — I started dataset-first and had to re-architect mid-project.

What's next

Google Places API for verified hours, a true “Open now”, and real photos.
